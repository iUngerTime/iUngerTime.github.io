Description

Have you ever tried to find a place to eat with someone and you just can't seem to agree on what to eat? Sitting together, both of you scrolling through restaurants and then showing each other your phone, hoping that the other person might want to go there as well. Or have you ever tried to make dinner plans with someone who doesn't know how to speak their mind and give a concrete decision quickly? Chicken Tinder was conceptualized for this specific problem!

Get on my website, get your group into the same lobby, and use a Tinder(ish) swiping process to find a restaurant match that is closest to you! This app is my senior project to graduate OIT and is still in development, feel free to visit the website to check the progress which will be finished by early April!
