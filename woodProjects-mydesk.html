My desk

Description

A beautiful, barn-door styled desk to increase productivity and workspace. This 63" x 29" beauty was my second project and boy was I excited to get rid of the old, and dark, "gamer" set-up that I had before.

I utilized a TON (about 70) pocket holes, my first lesson in over-engineering, on this project. I also refurnished the metal from my older desk instead of creating a wooden base. This desk is a heavy beast!
