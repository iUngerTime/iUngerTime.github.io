About Me

Born and raised in Texas, and moving to Washington at the age of 11. I have spent two halves of my life immersed in multiple types of culture. I cannot be defined by a stereotype as I fit into multiple at the same time. I am a nerd, gamer, sports player, gym rat, health nut, snowboarder, guitarist, (bad) artist, singer, carpenter, rock-climber, and more.

In my personal life I value delving into as many hobbies as I can to experience as much as I can and find out what I love most. Throughout my life I plan on delving into other hobbies such as sky-diving, travelling, hiking, MMA, culinary arts, and polyglot! Connect with my on my social media.

Currently, my professional life is developing. I plan on putting my hands to as many different projects as possible and trying out as much as I can. I am not yet experienced enough to know what I truly love or hate, so I keep myself open to as many possibilities as possible.
